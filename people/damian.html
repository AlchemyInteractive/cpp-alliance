Damian Jarek

Staff Engineer

During his 3 year long professional career, Damian Jarek has worked on a number of embedded projects most of them based on open source software, e.g. Yocto or OpenWRT. Most recently he has worked in the automotive industry on enabling real-time sensor data exchange between autonomous driving vehicles. Graduated in Automation & Robotics and Computer Science on the Wrocław University of Technology.
